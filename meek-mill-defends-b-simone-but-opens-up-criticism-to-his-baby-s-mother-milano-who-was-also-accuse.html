After B. Simone was called out by a Twitter user who says she directly copied multiple pages from her planner to create a manifestation book, Meek Mill is coming to her defense to those trying to cancel her. However, he opened up another can of worms by doing so.

The comedian was already in hot water for saying that she can’t date a man with a 9 to 5. So, when a photo of one of her journals busting at the seams went viral, she was hit with harsh criticism that eventually turned into an accusation that she’s plagiarized someone else’s work.

Meek took to Twitter to say that she shouldn’t be canceled especially because many white-owned brands steal from minorities without any consequences.
