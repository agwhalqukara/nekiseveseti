Natalie Viscuso is the Daughter of Millionaire Night Club Owner, Michael Viscuso

Natalie Viscuso was born on the 26th of March 1989 in Roswell, New Mexico. Her nationality is American, while her ethnicity is partly Caucasian. Natalie is the only child of her parents, Michael Viscuso and Tammie Kay Baumann.

Her mom was a businesswoman and architectural expert. Tammie ran her own company Expedite LA where she helped builders obtain permits and other needed documentation. She also worked for many architectural firms in Texas.

Tammie Kay passed away on 27th August 2017 as a result of cancer, and this devastated her daughter. Natalie still remembers her mom to this day and often posts sweet tributes to her on social media. She has described her mom as the strongest woman she knows and praised her incredible beauty, joy, and radiance even during her fight against cancer.

Natalie Viscuso’s father is Michael Viscuso, a wealthy entrepreneur. Michael owns several nightclubs and restaurants in the San Diego part of California, namely Entrada Restaurant and JJ Steakhouse. He is also the CEO of Covo La Jolla, a large, luxurious multi-purpose event center in downtown La Jolla, California.

Natalie Viscuso’s parents were never married, and she spent the first 15 years of her life living with her mom, aunt, and uncle in New Mexico. It was at the age of 15 that she relocated to San Diego, California, to live with her dad and his then girlfriend-turned-wife named Melissa.
