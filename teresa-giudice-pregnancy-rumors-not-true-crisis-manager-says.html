Teresa Giudice Pregnancy Rumors Not True, Crisis Manager Says

July 30, 2024
blog

Not so fast. Rumor spread this week that The Real Housewives of New Jersey star Teresa Giudice is expecting her fifth child, after InTouch published a cover story claiming that she is pregnant. 

Wendy Feldman, crisis manager for Teresa and Joe Giudice, tells Us Weekly, however, that there "is NO truth to the rumor" that the reality star is pregnant, adding that the story on Giudice, 42, is "false in every way."

"Teresa does not know if she is going to prison and would not have [a] baby, nor would anybody, under the circumstances described," Feldman continued. 

The magazine also claimed that Giudice is facing more than two years of jail time, with her husband Joe looking at four years. "The Judge in the matter of Teresa and Joe Giudice will determine the appropriate sentence once she has read all of the paperwork submitted and heard all of the arguments," Feldman said. "Clearly that has not yet happened."
