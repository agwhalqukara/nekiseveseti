Who is Jacob De La Hoya?

Oscar De La Hoya

American boxing promoter Oscar De La Hoya is a retired professional boxer who competed from 1992 to 2008. Both his father and grandfather boxed professionally in the 1960s, and his grandfather competed as an amateur in the 1940s.
