Peaky Blinders Season 6 Review:

Peaky Blinders Season 6 has received mixed reviews. Some critics have praised the season for its reflective nature and a deeper look into Tommy’s psyche. Screen Rant noted that the season is “notably slower and more contemplative than the seasons which have come before it” and that it “allows for a deeper look into Tommy’s grief-stricken and war-torn psyche.”

On the other hand, Polygon criticized the season for being “hobbled by death” and making its final season a punishment. Entertainment Daily UK praised the season for having more action than ever before.

Peaky Blinders Season 6 Rating:

“Peaky Blinders” Season 6 has achieved a solid rating of 7.5/10 on IMDb, indicating positive reception from viewers. On Metacritic, the season has garnered an approval rating of 85% based on 11 critic reviews, further affirming its positive critical reception.
