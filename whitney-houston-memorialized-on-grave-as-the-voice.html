Whitney Houston Memorialized on Grave as The Voice

blog

A headstone bearing Whitney Houston’s name was placed upon her New Jersey grave last week etched with the words “I Will Always Love You.”

These words are, of course, the title of and chorus to a song Houston made famous— though it was originally written and performed by Dolly Parton. Houston is buried in Westfield, New Jersey’s Fairview Cemetery, in the plot next to her father.

Houston’s daughter Bobbi Kristina Brown was engaged last week to Nick Gordon. Gordon was a fixture in the Brown-Houston household — so much so that Bobbi Kristina had to clarify that she and Gordon were not brothers or adopted siblings when announcing their engagement.
