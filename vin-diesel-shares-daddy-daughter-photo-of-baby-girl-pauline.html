Vin Diesel Shares Daddy-Daughter Photo of Baby Girl Pauline

All too cute, daddy Groot! Guardians of the Galaxy actor Vin Diesel shared the world's cutest daddy-daughter photo with his 4-month-old baby girl, Pauline, on Monday, Aug. 10.

"All love…" the burly action star, 48, wrote, alongside a beautiful candid of his little girl kissing daddy's forehead.

In the pic, baby Pauline affectionately grips Vin Diesel's earlobe with her tiny fingers as she looks up at him with fascination. The proud father, meanwhile, grins and squeezes his eyes shut as he cuddles his little one.

The Fast & Furious actor welcomed his baby girl with his longtime girlfriend, Paloma Jimenez, in March, and named little Pauline after his late friend and costar Paul Walker.

"There's no other person that I was thinking about as I was cutting this umbilical cord," Diesel told the Today show in March. "I just thought… I knew he was there and it felt like, you know, a way to keep his memory a part of my family and a part of my world."
