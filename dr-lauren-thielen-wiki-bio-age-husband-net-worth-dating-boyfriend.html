Career Beginnings

Right after graduation, now Dr. Lauren started pursuing her career as a veterinarian. Her academic qualifications have helped her climb the ladder of success quickly, as she was spotted by Doctor Susan Kelleher, so between 2013 and 2014, she did an internship as a part of Dr. Kelleher’s team at Broward Avian & Exotic Animal Hospital in Deerfield Beach, Florida, which was a great opportunity to gain knowledge and experience in the field of exotic animal medicine.

Avian Specialist

In 2014, Dr. Lauren joined the Veterinary Center for Birds & Exotics Animals in Bedford Hills, New York, and worked there for two years as a resident veterinarian, after which she became certified as an avian specialist. Afterwards, she returned to Dr. Kelleher’s team, and since then has been working there as an associate veterinarian. Additionally, she led the research of Macaws, long-tailed colorful parrots, at the Tambopata Research Center located in the heart of the Amazon rainforest in Peru, and currently serves in the position of the Marketing Chair for the Association of Exotic Mammal Veterinarians (AEMV). Besides that, Dr. is a member of both the American Veterinary Medical Association and the Association of Avian Veterinarians, helping to increase further her net worth.
