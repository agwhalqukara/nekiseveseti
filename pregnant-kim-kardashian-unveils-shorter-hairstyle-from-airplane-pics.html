Pregnant Kim Kardashian Unveils Shorter Hairstyle From Airplane: Pics

July 03, 2024
blog

Flying high on a new hairstyle! Pregnant Kim Kardashian—who's expecting her second child, a boy, with husband Kanye West—took to Instagram on Monday, Aug. 3, to chronicle her latest trim. And because she was on the move and clearly could not get enough of her new haircut, she even snapped a selfie inside an airplane bathroom!

The chop should come as no surprise. While Kardashian took the weekend to reveal the 'do, she did tease that it was coming this past Friday. "Officially OVER my long hair!!!! [Getting my hair cut] soon!!!" she wrote.

And cut she did! Squished inside the stall, the brunette beauty unveiled her fresh style (and a chocolate moto jacket) with a snapshot, which she simply captioned, "Airplane selfie." Kardashian shared "right before" and after pictures, too, one of which featured her tongue stuck out at the camera (and lots of fans standing outside her car).
