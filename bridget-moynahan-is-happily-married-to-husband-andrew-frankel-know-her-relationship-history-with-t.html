Bridget Moynahan relationship with Tom Brady

Moynahan began dating NFL quarterback Tom Brady in 2004. Brady has the most Super Bowl MVP awards and is one of only two players to win five Super Bowls. On December 14, 2006, a rep for Moynahan told People that the couple split in a statement that said,

“….(They) amicably ended their three-year relationship several weeks ago. We ask for your respect and consideration of their privacy…”

However, in an unexpected turn of events, Liz Smith of the New York Post on February 18, 2007, reported that the actress was pregnant with Brady. And according to her spokeswoman  Christina Papadopoulos, she was three months pregnant and “healthy and excited.”

On August 22, 2007, she gave birth to John Edward Thomas Moynahan in Santa Monica, California. John is Moynahan’s only child and Brady’s first-born since he has two other children with his supermodel wife, Gisele Bündchen.

Bridget Moynahan and husband, Andrew Frankel.

Moynahan met businessman Andrew Frankel through a friend. And on October 17, 2015, they got married in a private ceremony in the Hamptons. Frankel is the co-founder of Stuart Frankel & CO. Incorporation, a stock brokerage firm in New York.

He has three children of his own and is now the step-father to 10-year-old John Moynahan. In place of gifts, the newlyweds asked guests to donate to The Hole in Wall Gang Camp. The organization serves youth with cancer and other serious blood diseases.

Bridget Moynahan net worth and career.

After graduating from Longmeadow High School, Moynahan signed with a modeling agency in Springfield, Massachusetts. Her career kicked off by appearing in department-store catalogs in the area. She then moved to NYC at the age of 18 and began appearing in magazines like Vogue and Elle.

And within years she was featured on the cover of such top magazines. For instance, she made the cover of Elle in October 1993. She then started doing cosmetics advertising and began studying acting at Caymichael Patten Studio.

Her TV career began in 1999 when she debuted in Sex and the City as Natasha. Her role as Rachel in Coyote Ugly (2000) is considered her breakthrough role. She went on to feature in notable films and TV shows like The Sum of All Fears (2002), I, Robot (2004), Six Degrees (2006-2007), John Wick (2014) et cetera.
